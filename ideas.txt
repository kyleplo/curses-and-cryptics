Curses
- Curse of Obscuring - hides enchantment list other than self
- Curse of Immutability - prevents additional enchantments from being applied in an anvil
- Curse of Miasma (armor) - increases detection range
- Curse of Quixotism - hides durability bar
- Curse of Instability - has a chance of breaking when modified in anvil
- Curse of Impermanence - has a chance to be deleted when put in container

Smithing
- Transmogrification Smithing Template - changes texture/model to different item
- Glinting Smithing Template - overrides enchantment glint (possibly colors?)
- Veiling Smithing Template - hides armor model

Grindstone
- Whetstone - makes grindstone remove a singular random enchantment
- Blessed Whetstone - makes grindstone remove a singular random enchantment, could include curses
- Enchanted Blessed Whetstone - makes grindstone remove all curses and no other enchantments
